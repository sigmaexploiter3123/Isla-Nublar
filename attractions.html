Park Attractions

Safari Rides

Experience an unforgettable safari ride through the wild terrain of Isla Nublar. Our guided tours take you on a thrilling journey where you'll encounter lifelike dinosaur habitats and breathtaking landscapes. Hold on tight as you explore the park's most adventurous routes!

Dinosaur Exhibits

Our dinosaur exhibits offer an immersive experience, bringing you face-to-face with some of the most iconic species ever to roam the Earth. Detailed displays, interactive elements, and life-size models create a truly educational and exciting encounter with the prehistoric world.
