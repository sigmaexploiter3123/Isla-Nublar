Visitor Information

Plan Your Visit

Welcome to Isla Nublar! Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned explorer, our park offers a wealth of experiences for every guest. Here’s everything you need to know to plan your perfect visit.

Tickets: Purchase your tickets online or at the park entrance. We offer various packages including day passes, family discounts, and seasonal promotions.

Park Map: Download our interactive park map to navigate through the attractions, exhibits, and facilities with ease.

Schedules: Check our daily schedules for attractions, shows, and guided tours. We recommend arriving early to make the most of your day at Isla Nublar.

Parking: Ample parking is available on-site. For the best experience, consider using our shuttle service that connects the parking area to the main attractions.
