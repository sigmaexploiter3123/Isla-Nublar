Tyrannosaurus Rex

Tyrannosaurus Rex is famously known as the king of the dinosaurs. Roaming the late Cretaceous period, this apex predator featured one of the most powerful bites in the animal kingdom. With its robust skull and razor-sharp teeth, T. rex was capable of crushing bone and dispatching prey with incredible efficiency.

Velociraptor

Velociraptor, although much smaller than T. rex, was renowned for its agility, intelligence, and pack-hunting strategies. This swift predator thrived during the late Cretaceous period, using its slender, agile body and sharp claws to capture prey.

Recent research suggests that Velociraptor was feathered, lending it an even more dynamic appearance and hinting at its evolutionary link to modern birds. Its strategic hunting methods and high speed made it a particularly effective predator in its environment.
